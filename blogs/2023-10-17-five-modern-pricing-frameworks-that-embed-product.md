An analysis of five prevalent SaaS pricing models: plans with usage entitlements (Zapier, Retool), pay-as-you-go (OpenAI), usage volume buckets (AWS), unified credit systems (Confluent), and transaction-value percentages (Stripe). Each framework includes implementation details, advantages, and limitations for different business contexts.
